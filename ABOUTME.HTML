About Joy

I am Joy, a Youth Corp member serving in Enugu State and a proud graduate of the University of Lagos.

A dedicated individual passionate about technology, engineering, and making a difference in the world. My journey in tech began with a spark of curiosity, and over the years, I've honed my skills and grown both professionally and personally.

Currently, I'm deepening my knowledge as a student at AltSchool Africa, where I am driven by a passion for innovation and technology. My love for tech sparked during my third year at the University when I watched my friend explore cloud servers and cybersecurity. This experience ignited my curiosity and led me to pursue Cloud Engineering, with the goal of becoming a remarkable Cloud or DevOps Engineer who inspires others.

My Journey



From starting out as an Electrical Engineering intern to diving into cloud engineering, my journey is one of perseverance and passion. I've navigated through challenges, learned from experiences, and am now focused on using my skills to impact the world positively.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I run a small jewelry business, blending creativity with entrepreneurship. I find joy in making new meals, spending time at the beach, and singing karaoke with friends. My self-care involves cherishing alone time with music or books, and I thrive on connecting with new people, continuously expanding my horizons.
